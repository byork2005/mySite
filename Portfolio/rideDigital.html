Ride Digital

Ride Digital is a marketing agency in Overland Park, KS that offers a full suite of marketing services, website design/management and video production. They have two custom built website platforms that double as inventory and lead management systems. Ride develops, maintains and upgrades all platforms in house as well as facilitates the website builds itself within the platforms, wordpress or custom. Ride Digital is a child company of the Route 66 RV Network. The Ride development team builds and manages all applications and websites for the network as well.

As a developer for Ride Digital, I focus mainly on website builds and platform enhancements. We regularly use HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript and PHP, while working with mySQL, AJAX and Git. I have built/rebuilt at least 30 websites in my time with Ride Digital.
